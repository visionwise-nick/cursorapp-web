Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to InstanApp! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our AI app generation platform.

By using InstanApp, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Profile picture (optional)

2.2 Generated Content Data

To provide our core service, we process:

  • Your text prompts and app descriptions
  • App assets and code generated by our AI
  • App configuration settings

2.3 Usage Data

We collect information on how you interact with our service:

  • Time spent on the app
  • Features used
  • Error logs and performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide Service: Generate apps based on your prompts.
  • Improve AI Models: Refine our generation algorithms (using anonymized data).
  • Customer Support: Assist with technical issues.
  • Safety: Monitor for prohibited content generation.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest to protect your personal information.

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Cloud service providers (e.g., for hosting and database)
  • AI model providers (only the prompt content necessary for generation)

6. Children's Privacy

InstanApp is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
